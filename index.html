Welcome . . . .

To the new website of the London Ambulance Service Benevolent Fund

Feel free to navigate around the site and search for any information that may be helpful to you or send an instant message detailing any questions that you may have about the Benevolent Fund and we will reply ASAP.

This site has been set up to overcome the complexities and cost of communications with our membership and will be regularly updated to ensure that the latest information on any development or changes regarding the management of the Fund is available to our members at all times.

You may navigate our site via the Page Description buttons at the top of each page. If you click a link to another web site you may return to this site by use of the back button on your browser.
